Who are we?

Austech is an industry leading information technology service provider and hardware developer. We provide IT services for other companies or independent bodies, including contract work, reviews, and labour outsourcing. Austech is also a certified hardware manufacturer, with decades of experience, and ongoing research into nanotechnology.

Why Join Us?

Not only do you get to be part of one of the world's best IT service providers and researchers, but we care about your health, wellbeing, and growth. Thats why working at Austech means:

  • Excellent healthcare plans
  • Fantastic rates on paid leave
  • Modern, well kept, and safe workplaces
  • Work-at-home flexibility
  • Friendly and inclusive working environment (see our values)
  • World class training programs
  • Public transport assistance
  • Free childcare services
  • Company-paid yearly international travel
  • Up to $1000 bonus to spend on personal development
