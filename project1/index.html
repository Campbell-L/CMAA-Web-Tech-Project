Who are we?

Austech is an industry leading information technology service provider and hardware developer. We provide IT services for other companies or independant bodies, including contract work, reviews, and labour outsourcing. Austech is also a certified hardware manufacturer, with decades of experience, and ongoing research into nanotechonlogy.
